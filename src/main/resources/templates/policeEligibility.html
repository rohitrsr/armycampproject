Uttarakhand Police Eligibility Criteria Check

The Candidates who are going to apply for Uttarakhand Police SI/ Constable Recruitment 2020 can check the required Uttarakhand Police Constable Eligibility Criteria given below :-

Post Name Qualification
Constable Must be qualified 12th Class
Head Constable Should be qualified 12th Class
Sub Inspector (SI) Should be Posses Graduation Degree (In Any Stream)
Home Guard Should be qualified 10th Class
Assistant Sub Inspector (ASI) Graduation Degree (Any Stream)

Age Limit For Uttarakhand Police Bharti 2020

  • For Constable:- Candidates must have 18 years minimum and 22 Years Maximum age limit.
  • For ASI:- Minimum age should be 20 years and maximum age should be 28 years.
  • For ACP/ DSP Post:- Applicants must have 21 years minimum and 42 Years Maximum age limit.
  • Age relaxation will be applicable on the board norms.
