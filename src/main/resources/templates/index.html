Who we are?

Our center is situated in Gularbhoj,Uttarakhand since 2012. We provide physical and written exam training for army and police recruitments. We have vast experience of giving physical training to make our student ready for army and police physical/written test and other difficult challenges. We provide the environment which help our students adapt to any tough environment and succeed to excellance.
Our goal is to develop the quality in students , help them cross the hurdles and serve for their nation.
For joining our camp you can contact on the address and mobile number provided at the bottom.

  • Our Training Programs

    We have a custom structured training program which include circuit physical training which is combination of 100m,200m,400m, merathon race etc.. followed by written exam preparation. Performance of each student is examined on every scale to make our student better each day.

  • About Trainer

    Retired Subedar Inder Singh Rathour has served in Indian Army for 22 years. He was one of the finest trainer in Indian Army who has trained many new army joiners. Using his experience in army he is training students who have dreams to join army and police.

  • Training Fee

    Our nominal fee is 6000 rs/month which is combo of physical training, written preparation and accomdation.

  • Accomodation

    Hostel and food facility is available in the camp whose fees is covered in combo pack.
